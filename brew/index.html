Origin
Where it
grows.

Coffee originates from Ethiopia. Not symbolically — botanically. Every variety cultivated on every farm on Earth descends from wild Coffea arabica first found in the forests of this country. Kochere is a woreda in the Gedeo zone of Yirgacheffe — the highest-altitude coffee-growing area in Ethiopia, at 2,100 metres. At that altitude, the slow ripening season produces a density of flavour that no lower farm can replicate. The jasmine and bergamot notes are not added. They are grown. You are drinking coffee from the place coffee comes from, at the altitude it grows best, for €1 a cup. There is nothing comparable.

Craft
How it is
made.

AMG Coffee Export pulps the cherries within hours of picking. Fermented 36–48 hours in clean water tanks. Dried on raised beds for 10–15 days in direct Yirgacheffe sun — not on the ground, not in shade. Roasted in small batches in the Alps: light, taken to first crack at 196°C, stopped there. Rested 48 hours. Ground to medium for immersion. Sealed in unbleached kraft, nitrogen-flushed. Shipped within 7 days of roast. We name every step. Anyone who doesn't is hiding something.

— Three Decisions

Why this bean.
Why this cup.

01 · THE SOURCE
AMG Coffee Export. Kochere. Direct.

AMG Coffee Export has operated in Yirgacheffe since the cooperative system was built — working with smallholder farmers in Kochere woreda at 2,100 metres, where the altitude slows cherry ripening by weeks and concentrates flavour no lower farm can replicate. Washed process: cherries pulped within hours of picking, fermented 36–48 hours in clean water tanks, then dried on raised beds for 10–15 days in direct sun. Washed means clean. Every floral note in the cup is in the bean — nothing added, nothing hidden. EUDR-compliant. Traceable to the cooperative. We name them because anyone who doesn't is hiding something.

02 · THE TEMPERATURE
92–94°C. Not boiling.

Boiling water is 100°C. The floral aromatic compounds in Kochere that produce jasmine and bergamot on the palate are heat-volatile above 94°C — they scorch on contact and disappear from the cup. Letting the kettle rest for 45 seconds after boiling is not a preference. It is the instruction. The pause is the brewing.

03 · THE BAG
Kraft. Unbleached. Nitrogen-flushed.

The bag is unbleached kraft cellulose — no chlorine, no dioxin bleach, no chemical residue near the coffee. Nitrogen-flushed at packing to displace oxygen: the enemy of roasted coffee is not time, it is oxidation. Sealed, this bag holds peak flavour for 90 days. Once opened, 14. The bag composts in 8–12 weeks. Nothing in it is decorative.
