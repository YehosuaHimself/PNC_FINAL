The Rules We Follow
01
No ingredient named with a number.

Every ingredient in our bread has a name you can say aloud. Buckwheat. Psyllium husk. Alpine salt. If you cannot picture it, it is not in our bag. E-numbers, stabilisers, and "modified starch" do not belong in bread.

02
Priced at cost. Not at what the market will bear.

We charge what it costs to do it right — no more. €3 for five loaves. €1 per cup of Grade 1 coffee from 2,400 metres. The finest things in their category should not be reserved for the few who can afford a premium.

03
One origin. Always named. Never blended away.

The coffee in our pad comes from one place: Kochere, Ethiopia. Not a blend. Not a "single-origin mix." One farm region. One altitude. One flavour. You will taste jasmine, bergamot, and blueberry — because we did not blend them out for consistency.
