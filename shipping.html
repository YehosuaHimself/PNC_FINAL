Shipping & cancellation.

V1.0 · Effective MMXXVI · Last reviewed today

Where we ship

The European Union plus Switzerland and the United Kingdom. If you live somewhere else and want PNC, email hello@pncbread.love — we add countries based on where the demand sits.

How we ship

  • Boxes leave our bakery on Monday and Tuesday by DHL or DPD, depending on country.
  • Most addresses receive their box within 2–4 working days of dispatch.
  • Each sachet is vacuum-sealed; the dry mix is shelf-stable for 9 months unopened, 4 weeks once opened.
  • Tracking link arrives by email when your label is printed.

Shipping cost — Bread

  • Germany: €2.90 for a 5-loaf box · €5.80 for a 10-loaf box.
  • All other countries: €5.90 for a 5-loaf box · €11.80 for a 10-loaf box.
  • 20-loaf box: €11.60 in Germany · €23.60 for all other countries.

Shipping cost — Brew (coffee)

  • Germany: €2.90 flat — all sizes (7, 30, or 100 bags).
  • All other countries: €5.90 flat — all sizes.

Cancellation — one click, no email needed

You can cancel a subscription or pre-order from your account, anytime, with one click. We do not bury the cancel button. We do not require you to call. We do not "offer to pause your subscription" twelve times before letting you go.

If you'd rather email: cancel@pncbread.love. We answer within one working day. The subject line "cancel" is enough.

Before we charge you

Cancel any time, no money has moved.

After we charge but before the box ships

Cancel any time. We refund the full amount within 5 working days back to your card.

After your box has shipped

EU consumers have a statutory 14-day right of withdrawal on unopened boxes. Email cancel@pncbread.love with your order number and we'll send a return label. We refund within 14 days of receiving the box back.

If something is wrong with your box

Damaged in transit, sachet broken, mix smells off, batch number missing — tell us within 14 days at hello@pncbread.love. We replace or refund. Your choice. We don't ask for the box back.

The short version. Ships across Europe in 2–4 days. €4.90–€11.90 by country, free over 4 boxes. Cancel any time with one click — we don't make it hard.
